Certified Venues

Certified Venues in Japan

Places certified by Third Place Japan through our 7-axis evaluation. Each venue is recognized not for what it serves, but for how it makes you feel.

Flagship カフェ・スペシャルティコーヒー

Green Beans Coffee

渋谷, Japan

Green Beans Coffee, a specialty coffee shop in Shibuya, Tokyo, holds Third Place Japan Flagship certification — recognized for exceptional comfort and silence, making it the city's premier destination for solo time, focused work, and quiet reading.

Platinum カフェ・スペシャルティコーヒー

Maruaki Roastery Matsudai

渋谷, Japan

Maruaki Roastery Matsudai, a roastery café set within an 80-year-old converted townhouse in Shibuya's upscale Matsudai district, holds Third Place Japan Platinum certification for an experience that is genuinely unlike anywhere else in the city.

特別感・非日常性
Gold カフェ・スペシャルティコーヒー

Shinsen Coffeehouse

渋谷, Japan

Shinsen Coffeehouse, a self-roasting café tucked into a residential street near Shinsen Station, holds Third Place Japan Gold certification for its outstanding silence and privacy — a rare quiet pocket in the heart of Shibuya.

静寂性・プライバシー
Gold カフェ・スペシャルティコーヒー

Dagayasandou Coffee

渋谷, Japan

Dagayasandou Coffee, a specialty coffee shop in Shibuya's Dagayasandou neighbourhood, holds Third Place Japan Gold certification for its carefully designed space — where natural light, chosen materials, and a flexible layout create comfort for solo visitors and small groups alike.
